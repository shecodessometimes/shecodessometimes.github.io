CS Club, Hackathons, and CTFs

Sophomore year, I was the co-president of CRLS' Computer Science Club. We participated in hackathons, worked with making VR apps with Unity, and flew this awesome automatic drone. Freshman year I participated in MA Hacks III and Metrohacks Women, winning the Beginner division for the first and using the latter as a learnathon. Sophomore year I competed in MetroHacks IV and the Astropreneurship, Space Medicine, and the Dataverse Hackathon, in which I won first place for the latter. I was also a part of the CTF club sophomore year and competed in multiple CTFs such as HSCTF, picoCTF, and peaCTF.
