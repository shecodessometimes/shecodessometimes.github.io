Musicals

I enjoy listening to musical soundtracks such as The Sound of Music, Man of la Mancha, Hamilton, Dear Evan Hansen, and Hadestown. I love how stories can be portrayed by their music, and how the music reflects the change in mood and plot. I also love the beautiful harmonies that often crop up. I've only ever watched 4 musicals, one of which was on Broadway (the Lion King, when I was like 6), but the few shows that I've seen have absolutely blown me away. I've been in two musicals before, and hope to be in more.
