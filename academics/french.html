The very first story that we read in French was Belle et la Bete, or, as it is better known in English, Beauty and the Beast. Because Beauty and the Beast is a fairy tale, everyone had to write their own original story as one of the assignments. This is the fairy tale that I came up with. It centers around a cat and a dog, who get lost while sailing in a storm. The cat learns to confront his fears, and is able to save the dog from drowning.
