During D Term, we worked in groups of three to four people on the Apps for Good project, where each group makes an app to solve a problem. My group, Anvi, Akhi, and I, chose to make an app that would help people be more conscious of their carbon usage. It's called Carbon Truth, and we made it through Android Studio. You can find a demo of the app here, and below is a presentation summarizing our process and how to use the app.

Despite arguments over whether or not global warming is real, it comes as no doubt that the atmosphere is filled with more negative and dangerous heat now than ever before. Among the reasons for the earth warming so drastically are carbon emissions, and this is where our app comes in. CarbonTruth aims to help individual consumers track their carbon footprint in order for them to make the most environmentally conscious decisions. Our app will allow users to see their personal carbon footprints, as well as compare them to other users and to the worldwide averages. Our app can also be used to create healthy competition between large groups of people in order to enhance environmental consciousness. This app will be used on both Apple and Android platforms. This app targets individuals who wish to track their carbon usage in order to do their part in minimizing greenhouse effects.
