Hi there! My name is Julia Rasmussen and I am currently a senior at Northeastern University studying Electrical Engineering and Music Technology. Here at Northeastern I am involved in NUSound (our chapter of the Acoustic Society of America) as a Workshop Co-ordinator, and NUStage (a fully student run musical theater stage company). I dabble in music production and songwriting, though I have to get better at both before I release anything. I went to Massachusetts Academy of Math and Science my junior and senior years of high school, and I also went to Cambridge Rindge and Latin my first two years of high school.
