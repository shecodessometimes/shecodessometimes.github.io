For the last two labs of my Circuits and Signals class, we designed an ECG sensor in pairs, and then tested the sensors on each other. We first filtered the incoming signal from the electrodes with a high and low pass op-amp filter to filter out noise and extraneous signals. This data was then fed to MATLAB using an A/D, and was then further analyzed in order to determine the bpm. The full paper can be found below.
